FactPro

It is a new online service dedicated to automatically organize your electronic invoices. We will reduce the time you lose a day having to take your accounting and visit your accountant hours just minutes from any device.

How does it work?

The basic process is as follows:

  • You register as a user and verify your email account.
  • In your account you can find everything perfectly organized by invoices sent and received, along with a section of your accounting. The latter allows you to select the time period you want to consult with an itemized summary of your accounting, ready for you to file any tax return.

You will not have to re-visit your accountant!
